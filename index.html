Note on Undernutrition

Undernutrition in children during growth periods can be due to several factors including inadequate dietary intake, poor absorption of nutrients, and increased nutrient requirements. It's crucial to ensure children receive balanced meals to support their physical and mental development.

Welcome to the Child Nutrition Guide

Your resource for healthy eating habits and meal plans for children.

Nutrition Tips

  • Include a variety of foods in your child's diet.
  • Focus on whole grains, lean proteins, and fresh fruits and vegetables.
  • Limit sugary drinks and snacks.
  • Encourage regular physical activity.
  • Stay hydrated with plenty of water.
